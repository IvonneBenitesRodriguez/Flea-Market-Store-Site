About

After years of looking for the idea for a business, Ana and me found a very interesting idea which can help other people like you who are constantly moving or looking for a change. We believe that change does not come only when you move to another city or another country, the change can come simply with the idea of making better your space, the space where you daily work or live, making beautiful the space or things you are surrounded with is important. Giving value to things you used one time and putting them in sale not only helps you to get a small money, you are helping to the environment, you are not part of the consumption era. Visit Flea Markets you will find not only treasures but stories.
